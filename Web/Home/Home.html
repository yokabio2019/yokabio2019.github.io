    • Abstract

    Enzymes are essential substance for all living. For example, the reason why we can take in nutrients through meals is that what we take is broken down by an enzymatic reaction and the nutrients can be absorbed into our body.

    However, enzymes have some disadvantages. One of the disadvantages is that there are only a few active centers that exist in a bigger enzyme molecule and lowing the enzymatic reaction. To overcome the problem, we came up with the idea to make a new mechanism that does the activity the same as the active center of enzymes to increase the enzymatic reactions’ activity. The method is by connecting the end side of two DNA origamis. Each side of the DNA origami is respectively binding with a substrate and catalyst allowing them to react forcibly when the DNA origami is folded. This mechanism makes non-specific of two molecules could react effectively.
