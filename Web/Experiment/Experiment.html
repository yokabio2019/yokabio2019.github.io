    2. Annealing

    3. Fluorescence intensity measurement

      4. Fluorescence intensity was measured at 1 minute intervals using a fluorescence spectrophotometer (JASCO: FP-8300).

    The reaction was confirmed using FRET. FAM and BHQ1 is modified respectively to the 5’ end of A and 5’ end of B. In the OFF state, A and B become double-stranded and absorb fluorescence. In the ON state, A is in a single-strand state cause it to emit fluorescence.
